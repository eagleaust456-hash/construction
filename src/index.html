From Refugee Roots to Building Futures

My name is Munyentwali Augustin. Born a refugee, I discovered hope and strength in building. Today, I dedicate my life to constructing safe, beautiful houses that bring dignity and pride to communities.

Nyabiheke Refugee Camp

I was born in 2005, in a refugee camp. My mother fled while pregnant with me. Growing up, I understood the pain of displacement, but also the power of resilience. Construction is more than work to me, it’s a calling. It means giving families what we lacked: stability, safety, and dignity through good houses. Now, I share my journey with the world: from refugee to builder of hope.
